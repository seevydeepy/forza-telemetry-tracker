# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be direct, specific, and respectful.
- Critique ideas, code, tests, and documentation on their technical merits.
- Assume public comments may be read by users with different levels of experience.
- Keep issue and pull request discussions focused on reproducing, understanding, and improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to refocus.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to disclose private telemetry data, local game files, credentials, or personal paths.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block participants when behavior harms the project or its users. If you need to report conduct privately, contact a maintainer through a private channel instead of opening a public issue.
